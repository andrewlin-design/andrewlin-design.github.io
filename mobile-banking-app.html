Mobile Banking App

Designed a next-generation mobile banking experience focused on financial wellness and intuitive money management.

Client

Financial Institution

Duration

4 months

Role

Senior UX Designer

Team

Product Manager, iOS Developer, Android Developer

Project Overview

Traditional banking apps often focus on basic transactions but fail to help users understand and improve their financial health. This project aimed to create a comprehensive mobile banking experience that combines everyday banking with financial wellness tools.

The challenge was to design an interface that could handle complex financial data while remaining simple and approachable for users of all financial literacy levels.

Design Process

User Research

Through interviews with 20 bank customers, we discovered that users wanted more than just account balances - they needed tools to understand spending patterns, set financial goals, and make informed decisions about their money.

Key Features Designed

  • Smart Dashboard: Real-time financial overview with spending insights
  • Goal Setting: Visual progress tracking for savings and spending goals
  • Spending Analysis: AI-powered categorization and trend analysis
  • Quick Actions: One-tap access to frequent banking tasks
