E-commerce Platform Redesign

Complete redesign of a major e-commerce platform, improving conversion rates by 35% and reducing cart abandonment by 28%.

Client

E-commerce Company

Duration

6 months

Role

Lead UX Designer

Team

Product Manager, Frontend Developer, Backend Developer

Project Overview

The e-commerce platform was experiencing significant issues with user engagement and conversion rates. Customer feedback consistently pointed to a confusing checkout process, inconsistent design patterns, and poor mobile experience. The company needed a complete redesign to compete effectively in the market.

My role as Lead UX Designer involved conducting comprehensive user research, designing new user flows, creating a cohesive design system, and collaborating closely with the development team to ensure successful implementation.

The Challenge

Complex Checkout Flow

The existing checkout process had 7 steps with multiple form fields, causing high abandonment rates.

Inconsistent Design Patterns

Different sections of the site used varying button styles, colors, and layouts, confusing users.

Poor Mobile Experience

Mobile sales were significantly lower than desktop, with users reporting difficulty navigating on smaller screens.

Research & Discovery

I began with extensive user research to understand pain points and opportunities. This included:

  • User Interviews: Conducted 15 interviews with existing customers to understand their shopping journey
  • Analytics Analysis: Analyzed user behavior data to identify drop-off points
  • Competitive Analysis: Studied successful e-commerce platforms to identify best practices
  • Usability Testing: Tested the current platform with 8 users to observe struggles firsthand

Key insights revealed that users valued speed, trust, and clarity above all else. The checkout process was the primary source of frustration, with users abandoning carts due to unexpected costs and complicated forms.

Design Solution

Streamlined Checkout Process

I redesigned the checkout flow from 7 steps down to 3 simple stages: Information, Payment, and Confirmation. This involved consolidating form fields, implementing guest checkout options, and providing clear progress indicators.

Comprehensive Design System

Created a complete design system with consistent typography, color palette, button styles, and component library. This ensured visual consistency across all platform sections and made future updates easier.

Mobile-First Responsive Design

Implemented a mobile-first approach with touch-friendly interfaces, optimized loading times, and simplified navigation specifically designed for mobile users.

Results & Impact

35%
Increase in Conversion Rate
28%
Reduction in Cart Abandonment
45%
Improvement in Mobile Sales

The redesign exceeded all success metrics within the first quarter after launch. Customer satisfaction scores increased significantly, and the streamlined checkout process became a competitive advantage for the platform.

Key Learnings

This project reinforced the importance of user-centered design and iterative testing. The biggest breakthrough came from simplifying rather than adding features. Sometimes the best design solution is removing unnecessary complexity.

The collaborative relationship with the development team was crucial for success. Regular check-ins and shared understanding of user goals enabled us to make real-time adjustments during implementation.
