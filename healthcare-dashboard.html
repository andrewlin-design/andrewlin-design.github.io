Healthcare Provider Dashboard

Comprehensive dashboard for healthcare providers to manage patient data, appointments, and clinical workflows with 40% reduction in task completion time.

Client

Healthcare Technology Company

Duration

8 months

Role

UX Designer

Team

Product Manager, Full-stack Developer, Healthcare Consultant

The Challenge

Healthcare providers were struggling with outdated systems that required multiple applications to complete routine tasks. The fragmented workflow was causing delays in patient care and administrative burden on medical staff.

The goal was to create a unified dashboard that could handle patient management, appointment scheduling, clinical notes, and reporting while ensuring HIPAA compliance.

Research Insights

  • Workflow Analysis: Shadowed 12 healthcare providers to understand daily routines
  • Pain Point Identification: Mapped current system limitations and user frustrations
  • Compliance Requirements: Worked with legal team to ensure all privacy regulations were met

Design Solution

Modular Dashboard Architecture

Created a flexible dashboard system where users can customize their workspace based on their role and daily needs. This approach accommodated different medical specialties and workflow preferences.

Streamlined Patient Flow

Designed intuitive patient management tools that reduced the number of clicks needed to access critical information from 8 to 3 clicks on average.
